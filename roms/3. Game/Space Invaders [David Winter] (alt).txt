Space Invaders, by: David Winter

The well known game. Destroy the invaders with your ship. Shoot
with 5, move with 4 and 6. Press 5 to begin a game.

To start:     5      start game

To play:      5      shoot
              4      left
              6      right

Chip-8 Key  ->  On your keyboard
1 2 3 C         1 2 3 4
4 5 6 D         Q W E R
7 8 9 E         A S D F
A 0 B F         Z X C V
